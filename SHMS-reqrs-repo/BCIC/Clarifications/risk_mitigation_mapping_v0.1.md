# Appendix F: Risk Mitigation Mapping

## R-01 -- Undetected requirement incongruities

**Mitigation Mechanism:** Incongruous Requirements Checklist (IRC)\
**BCIC Stage Supported:** Breakdown\
**Outcome:** Systematic identification of ambiguous, compound, and
incomplete requirements

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-02 -- Clarification deadlock or stakeholder non-commitment

**Mitigation Mechanism:** Account Manager facilitation and escalation\
**BCIC Stage Supported:** Clarify\
**Outcome:** Improved stakeholder access and resolution of priority
ambiguities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-03 -- Policy and compliance ambiguity

**Mitigation Mechanism:** Account Manager coordination with policy
stakeholders\
**BCIC Stage Supported:** Clarify\
**Outcome:** Reduced regulatory uncertainty prior to interpretation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-04 -- Premature commitment to SRS structure

**Mitigation Mechanism:** Central requirements repository\
**BCIC Stage Supported:** Interpret / Categorise\
**Outcome:** Prevented early design assumptions and preserved controlled
incompleteness

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-05 -- Loss of traceability during revision

**Mitigation Mechanism:** Atomic requirement storage in repository\
**BCIC Stage Supported:** Categorise\
**Outcome:** Maintained traceability and consistency across revised
requirements
