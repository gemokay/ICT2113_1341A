# Appendix G: Risk Identification

## R-01 -- Requirements Quality

**Description:** Ambiguous, compound, or incomplete requirements may
remain undetected during Breakdown\
**Source:** Project Specification

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-02 -- Stakeholder

**Description:** Stakeholders may be unable or unwilling to commit to
clarification decisions\
**Source:** Clarify activities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-03 -- Compliance

**Description:** Data retention and access control requirements may
conflict with PDPA obligations\
**Source:** Regulatory constraints

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-04 -- Process

**Description:** Delays in clarification may stall Interpretation and
Categorisation\
**Source:** BCIC dependency

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## R-05 -- Scope

**Description:** Additional requirements may be introduced during
clarification discussions\
**Source:** Stakeholder meetings
